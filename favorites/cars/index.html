    BMW 507

    BMW 507 is a roadster produced in Germany by the BMW concern from 1956 to 1959. The car was unveiled at the 1955 Frankfurt Motor Show . The car was BMW's answer to its main competitor Mercedes-Benz and its 300SL model . The creation of the machine was entrusted to the young designer Albrecht von Goertz.
